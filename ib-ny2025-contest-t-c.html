1. Overview

2. Calculating results

  • 2.1. The Partners whose clients receive the most raffle tickets during the Octa New Year promotional campaign will become the winners of the Contest.
    • 2.1.1. The link to the Octa New Year promotional campaign: https://octamarkets.net/landing/new-year-2025

  • 2.4. The raffle tickets received by Valid New Unique Traders in the duration of the Contest will be counted as doubled.
  • 2.5. A Valid New Unique Trader is a trader who must simultaneously meet the following conditions:
  • 2.5.1. Has never previously traded on a real account with the Company;
  • 2.5.2. Has never previously worked with the Company under IB reference;
  • 2.5.3. Has closed at least five (5) Valid Orders within the Achievement Period;
  • 2.5.4. Has passed the KYC procedure and obtained verified account status on the Company’s site.

      1. Terms and interpretation

      • 1.1. Client is an individual or a legal entity registered on the Octa Markets Incorporated (hereinafter—‘Company’) website.
      • 1.2. Introducing Broker (hereinafter—‘IB’) is the Client whose application for the IB status submitted via the Company’s website was approved by the Company.
      • 1.3. Referral Link is a special link to the Company’s website containing a unique IB identifier. The Referral Link is the main means of attracting and tracking the Clients by the IB.
      • 1.4. ID is the IB’s unique identification number.
      • 1.5. IB Commission is the amount payable by the Company to the IB for their clients’ trading operations.
      • 1.6. Wallet is a special account dedicated to transactions and money settlements, onto which the IB Commission is credited.
      • 1.7. Active Сlient is a type of Client’s profile on the Company’s website for a Client whose trading accounts cumulatively hold funds of 100 USD or more and have at least five Valid Orders closed within the last 30 days from the applicable date.
      • 1.8. Valid Order is a trade compliant with all the following conditions:
        • 1.8.1. the trade lasted for 180 seconds or more
        • 1.8.2. the difference between Open Price and Close Price of the order equals or exceeds 30 points (3 pips in 4-digit precision terms)
        • 1.8.3. the order was not opened or closed by means of Partial Close and/or Multiple Close.
